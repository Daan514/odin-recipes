Dutch Hutspot

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of white sandwich bread
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • 2½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 3 pounds 80 to 85% lean ground beef
  • 8 hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to a high heat
  2. Remove the crust from your bread and cut it into ¼-inch pieces
  3. In a large bowl, mash the bread and milk together with a fork until it forms a chunky paste
  4. Add the salt, pepper, garlic, Worcestershire sauce and ketchup in the bowl and mix it all together
  5. Add the ground beef in the bowl and break the meat up with your hands
  6. Mix everything together until evenly combined
  7. Divide the mixture into 8 equal portions and form compact balls
  8. Flatten the balls into ¾-inch patties about 4½ inches across
  9. Form a slight depression in the center of each patty
  10. Oil the grilling grates
  11. Grill the burgers, covered, until nicely browned on the first side, 2 to 4 minutes
  12. Flip burgers and continue cooking for a few minutes more until desired doneness is reached
  13. Toast the buns
  14. Serve your burgers
